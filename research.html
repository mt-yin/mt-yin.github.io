Cardiovascular Stent Design


Cardiovascular stents are miniature tube-like structures that can be inserted into arteries to support blood vessels and prevent artery collapsing. By tuning critical dimensions of the stent structure, clinical performance may be either improved or sacrificed.

Finite Element Simulation


To evaluate stent performance, finite element models were built to simulate mechanical status of stents during implementation and in-vivo period. Simulation results provide information of stents such as stress, strain and stent critical dimensions. The information will be the basis of further analysis of stent performance.

Fatigue Resistance Analysis


Suffered from the repetitive pressure changing and alternating deformation caused by blood pressure and daily activities of patients, stents have risk of fatigue failure. By shifting the concentrated strain and re-distributing them along the bar arm structure, fatigue safety factor of stents increased to almost 2.5 times that of standard stents.

Delta Robot


The robot system comprises of a delta robot, a camera and a set of fixture. Inverse kinematic model was built in Simulink to analise robot arm working space and to control position of end effector. Image processing was done with Matlab to idnetify the positions of objects with defferent colors therefore end effector of robot could move to them automatically.

DC Motor Control


LabVIEW and CompactRIO were used to send swept sine signal to DC motors for system ID. Based on robust control theory, controllers were calculated and determined in Matlab then implemented in LabVIEW program to control motor position.

Micro Controller


Micro processor was used for system integration in the elevator controller project. The elevator control system comprises of a LED display for floor indication, a set of buutons to control the termination floor, motors with drivers to pull the elevator mechanism and a micro processor to integrate modules and control the elevator operation.

Measuring Instrument


Laser Doppler interferometer was used to measure natural frequency of the probe of scanning tunnelling microscope (STM) and evaluate the efficiency of our micro devices.

Micro Power Generator


With piezoelectrical material, micro structures have ability to generate oscillating electric field during vibration. By collecting the electric power from integrated array of those structures, the micro devices could be used as power generators while attached on external oscillation resources.
